Who We Are

CityPAC is an independent, bipartisan, pro-Israel political action committee dedicated to strengthening the U.S.–Israel relationship.

Our Mission

CityPAC works to educate, engage, and empower the Chicago Jewish community to participate directly in the political process in support of a strong, enduring U.S.–Israel relationship.

Who We Are

Unique to Chicago, CityPAC is an all-volunteer organization that empowers young men and women to get involved in politics, learn about Israel, and participate in meaningful social and educational programming.

Through its work, CityPAC not only strengthens Congressional support for Israel, but also develops the next generation of Jewish civic leaders.

Our History

Founded in 1984, CityPAC has been led by hundreds of committed Jewish Chicagoans in their 20s, 30s, and early 40s who share one overriding goal: supporting candidates who believe in a strong and enduring U.S.–Israel relationship.

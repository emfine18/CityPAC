Who We Are

CityPAC is a Chicago-based, independent, bipartisan political organization dedicated to strengthening the U.S.–Israel relationship by supporting Congressional leaders who share our commitment to Israel’s security, democracy, and future.

Our Mission

Founded in 1984, CityPAC brings together generations of engaged Chicagoans in their 20s, 30s, and 40s who believe that a strong U.S.–Israel relationship is vital to American interests and democratic values.

We work to educate, engage, and empower civic leaders while supporting bipartisan candidates who advocate for Israel’s security and a shared commitment to democracy.

What We Stand For

Bipartisanship

CityPAC proudly supports leaders from both parties who demonstrate a commitment to the U.S.–Israel relationship.

Community Leadership

We cultivate the next generation of civic leaders by creating meaningful opportunities for political engagement.

Impact & Integrity

As an all-volunteer organization, CityPAC is driven by impact, accountability, and responsible political advocacy.

Our Impact

Over four decades, CityPAC has helped sustain strong Congressional support for Israel by backing candidates who understand the importance of a secure, democratic Jewish state.

Through education, advocacy, and direct political engagement, we continue to play a unique role in Chicago’s civic and political landscape.

Get Involved

Whether you’re new to political advocacy or a seasoned leader, CityPAC offers meaningful ways to engage, learn, and make a difference.

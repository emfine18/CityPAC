About CityPAC

CityPAC is an independent, bipartisan, pro-Israel political action committee (PAC) dedicated to strengthening the U.S.–Israel relationship.

Who We Are

Chicago's Pro-Israel Community

Unique to Chicago, CityPAC is an all-volunteer organization that empowers young men and women to get directly involved in the political process, to learn about and support Israel, and to plan and participate in social and educational programs important to the pro-Israel community.

Through its work, CityPAC not only helps to maintain strong and steadfast Congressional support for Israel, but it also provides ongoing, real-world leadership opportunities for the Jewish community in Chicago.

Our Mission

Educate. Engage. Empower.

CityPAC exists to educate, engage, and empower the Chicago Jewish community to participate directly in the political process in support of a strong, enduring U.S.–Israel relationship.

We believe that bipartisan support for Israel is essential to both nations' security and prosperity. By connecting young professionals with candidates and elected officials who share our values, we ensure that pro-Israel voices are heard in Washington.

What We Do

Making an Impact

CityPAC provides meaningful opportunities for members to engage with the political process and build lasting connections within the pro-Israel community.

  • Support pro-Israel Congressional candidates
  • Host educational briefings and events
  • Organize social and networking programs
  • Develop future community leaders
  • Build bipartisan political relationships
  • Connect members with elected officials
Since 1984

Our History

Since 1984, CityPAC—led by the efforts of hundreds of committed Jewish Chicagoans in their 20s, 30s, and early 40s—has had one overriding mission: support candidates for and members of Congress that share the PAC's commitment to a strong and enduring U.S.–Israel relationship.

Over four decades, we have grown into one of Chicago's most impactful pro-Israel organizations, building relationships with lawmakers from both parties and across all 50 states. Our all-volunteer model ensures that every dollar raised goes directly toward supporting candidates who stand with Israel.

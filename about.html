About CityPAC

Mission

CityPAC is an independent, bipartisan, pro-Israel political action committee (PAC) dedicated to strengthening the U.S.–Israel relationship.

Unique to Chicago, CityPAC is an all-volunteer organization that empowers young men and women to get directly involved in the political process, to learn about and support Israel, and to plan and participate in social and educational programs important to the pro-Israel community.

Through its work, CityPAC not only helps to maintain strong and steadfast Congressional support for Israel, but it also provides ongoing, real-world leadership opportunities for the Jewish community in Chicago.

Our Priorities

U.S.–Israel Alliance

Ensuring unwavering bipartisan Congressional support for Israel’s security, legitimacy, and right to self-defense.

Bipartisan Engagement

Supporting candidates from both political parties who share CityPAC’s commitment to a strong and enduring U.S.–Israel relationship.

Next Generation Leadership

Empowering young Jewish professionals to engage directly in the political process through education, advocacy, and leadership opportunities.

Political Impact

Directly contributing to pro-Israel candidates and strengthening Congressional coalitions that support Israel.

History

Since 1984, CityPAC — led by the efforts of hundreds of committed Jewish Chicagoans in their 20s, 30s, and early 40s — has had one overriding mission: to support candidates for and members of Congress who share the PAC’s commitment to a strong and enduring U.S.–Israel relationship.

CityPAC has contributed more than $1.4 million to over 400 pro-Israel Congressional candidates from both political parties and from all 50 states.

Paid for by CityPAC and not authorized by any candidate or candidate committee. Contributions to CityPAC are not deductible for federal income tax purposes.

Who Can Join?

CityPAC offers Chicago professionals who are passionate about politics and Israel a host of hands-on, non-bureaucratic opportunities to get involved and lead. CityPAC members must meet the following criteria:

  • Be a young adult who shares a passion for Israel and for strengthening the U.S.–Israel relationship
  • Attend at least two political events with CityPAC each year
  • Make a financial contribution to CityPAC each year
Membership

Membership Levels

CityPAC offers three membership tiers designed for young professionals at different stages of their careers. Each level provides access to our events, educational programming, and the opportunity to be part of Chicago's pro-Israel political community.

Lake Shore Society
$360 / year

Designed for young professionals in their 20s looking to engage with CityPAC, attend events, and build leadership skills in the pro-Israel community.

Gold Coast Society
$720 / year

For professionals in their 30s seeking deeper involvement through programming, networking, and meaningful political engagement with CityPAC.

Chai Society
$1,080 / year

CityPAC's leadership tier for its most engaged members, offering exclusive access, high-level events, and national pro-Israel political opportunities.

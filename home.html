ABOUT US

Founded in 1984, CityPAC unites generations of dedicated Jewish Chicagoans in their 20s, 30s, and 40s around a single mission: supporting Congressional leaders who champion a strong and enduring U.S.–Israel relationship.

Unique to Chicago, CityPAC is an independent, bipartisan, all-volunteer organization sustaining vital Congressional support for Israel while empowering young leaders to engage directly in the political process.
